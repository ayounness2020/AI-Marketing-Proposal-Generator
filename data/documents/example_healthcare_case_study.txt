CASE STUDY
Agency: DigitalEdge Marketing
Client: MedCare Clinics Egypt
Project Duration: 6 months (2024)

Executive Summary
-----------------
MedCare Clinics approached DigitalEdge to grow patient bookings across their 5 Cairo locations.
Within 6 months we increased online appointment bookings by 187% and reduced cost-per-booking from EGP 450 to EGP 185.

Client Background
-----------------
MedCare Clinics is a private healthcare chain with 5 locations in Cairo (Nasr City, Maadi, Zamalek, New Cairo, 6th October).
Services include general medicine, dermatology, cardiology, and paediatrics.
Prior to the engagement, 70% of patient acquisition came from walk-ins and word-of-mouth.

Business Objectives
-------------------
1. Increase online appointment bookings by 100% within 6 months.
2. Build brand recognition in premium healthcare segment.
3. Attract patients from higher-income demographics.
4. Expand social media following on Facebook and Instagram.

Marketing Strategy
------------------
We deployed a full-funnel approach targeting patients at three stages:
- Awareness: Educational content marketing, health tips, doctor profiles
- Consideration: Retargeting campaigns with patient testimonials and service differentiators
- Conversion: Google Search ads targeting high-intent keywords (e.g. "best dermatologist Cairo")

SEO Strategy
------------
- Targeted 85 medical keywords across all specialties
- Created dedicated landing pages for each clinic location
- Produced 40 health education articles in Arabic and English
- Built local SEO citations for all 5 locations
- Result: 145% increase in organic search traffic; 32 keywords ranking in Google Top 5

Paid Ads Strategy
-----------------
Google Ads:
- Search campaigns targeting medical procedure keywords
- Display remarketing to website visitors
- Budget: EGP 18,000/month
- Result: 3.8% average CTR; EGP 210 average CPA

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram):
- Health awareness carousel ads
- Doctor spotlight video ads
- Appointment booking objective campaigns
- Budget: EGP 12,000/month
- Result: 2.3% conversion rate; reach of 280,000 unique users/month

Social Media Strategy
---------------------
- 45 posts/month across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn
- Weekly health tip videos featuring MedCare doctors
- Patient testimonial reels (with consent)
- Monthly health awareness campaigns (e.g. Diabetes Awareness Month)
- Result: Facebook followers grew from 8,200 to 31,400 (+283%)

Results
-------
Metrics at the end of Month 6 vs. baseline:

| Metric                        | Before   | After    | Change  |
|-------------------------------|----------|----------|---------|
| Monthly Online Bookings       | 312      | 896      | +187%   |
| Cost Per Booking (EGP)        | 450      | 185      | -59%    |
| Website Monthly Traffic       | 4,200    | 11,800   | +181%   |
| Organic Search Traffic        | 1,100    | 2,695    | +145%   |
| Facebook Followers            | 8,200    | 31,400   | +283%   |
| Instagram Followers           | 3,100    | 14,900   | +381%   |
| Google Ads CTR                | 1.9%     | 3.8%     | +100%   |

KPIs
----
- Primary: Online appointment bookings (Target: +100%. Achieved: +187%)
- Secondary: Cost per booking (Target: <EGP 250. Achieved: EGP 185)
- Brand: Social media growth (Target: +150% followers. Achieved: +283%)

Pricing
-------
Monthly Retainer for MedCare: EGP 28,000/month
Ad Spend Managed: EGP 30,000/month
Total 6-Month Investment: EGP 348,000

Return On Investment:
- Each new patient generates average EGP 1,200 in revenue
- 584 incremental new patients over 6 months = EGP 700,800 incremental revenue
- ROI: ~101% on total marketing investment

Recommendations
---------------
Based on the success of this campaign, we recommend MedCare:
1. Expand Google Ads to include Alexandria locations.
2. Launch a patient loyalty programme with email marketing.
3. Invest in YouTube health education channel.
4. Introduce Arabic-language chatbot for appointment booking.

Next Steps
----------
MedCare renewed contract for 12 months with expanded scope including email marketing and WhatsApp automation.
