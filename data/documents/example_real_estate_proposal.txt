MARKETING PROPOSAL
Prepared by: DigitalEdge Marketing Agency
Client: Nile Towers Real Estate
Date: Q1 2025

Executive Summary
-----------------
DigitalEdge proposes a comprehensive 6-month digital marketing programme for Nile Towers Real Estate
designed to increase qualified lead generation by 40% and reduce cost-per-lead by 25%.
Our strategy combines SEO, targeted paid advertising, and content marketing
to position Nile Towers as the premier real estate developer in Greater Cairo.

Client Background
-----------------
Nile Towers Real Estate is a mid-tier property developer operating primarily in New Cairo and Heliopolis.
Founded in 2010, the company has delivered 12 residential projects.
They face increasing competition from larger developers with bigger marketing budgets.
Their current digital presence is limited to a basic website with low organic traffic.

Business Objectives
-------------------
1. Generate 150 qualified buyer leads per month by month 3.
2. Increase website traffic by 60% within 90 days.
3. Build a recognisable brand in the Cairo luxury apartment segment.
4. Achieve a cost-per-lead below EGP 800.
5. Expand reach to Egyptian expat buyers in Gulf countries.
