Pricing Models
--------------
We offer three engagement models:

1. Monthly Retainer (Recommended)
   - Fixed monthly fee covering all services
   - Best for ongoing marketing relationships
   - 3-month minimum commitment

2. Project-Based
   - Fixed price for a defined deliverable
   - Examples: website launch campaign, product launch, event marketing
   - Suitable for one-off projects

3. Performance-Based (Select Clients Only)
   - Base retainer + performance bonus
   - Bonus tied to agreed KPIs (CPL, bookings, revenue)
   - Available after 3-month retainer track record

KPIs
----
We guarantee to report on (not guarantee performance):
- Leads generated
- Cost per lead
- Website traffic
- Conversion rates
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
- Social media growth

Contract Terms
